Introduction

Hello my name is Taavi Korhonen also known online as Tabbe.

Some of you from Kajaani might know me as being one of those guys with yellow hats.

I graduated Kajaani University of Applied Sciences in december of 2023 with a Bachelor's Degree in Business Information Technology.

While studying at Kamk I made my thesis on making & designing diegetic HUD for a mecha game in Unreal Engine 5.

When it comes to game development I mostly like to programm user interfaces. This doesn't mean I cannot handle other areas of game programming.
Recently I have been doing a solo project where I try to strenghten myself on areas where I am weak.

On the side of doing video games I also enjoy playing them and especially hunting achievements. But my life doesn't revolve only around gaming. Cooking good food & listening music is also close to my heart.

On this site you will find every project & jam I have been part of even if they are unreleased.
And if this website seems a bit scuffed that is because I'm still learning HTML & CSS. I'm no way a professional but I think I did a good job.
