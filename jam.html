Blind in Tuonela

Released: Itch.io

Platform: Pc

Engine: Unity

Time: 48hr (2024)


Blind in tuonela was done during a jam that was hosted at KAMK by IDGA Kajaani.

The idea of the game is stolen from the game Scanner Sombre, but with a twist that no one that made Blind in Tuonela had played it.

This is also my first time doing a project/jam that has steam multiplayer.

Overwiev


I tinkered a lot with optimization around the dots and how they change color depending on what kind of object they touch.

I also made the weapon switching script, and visuals for the menus.

Item interaction was also done by me.

OINK OINK BLASTER

Released: Itch.io

Platform: Pc

Engine: Godot

Time: 48hr (2024)


Oink Oink Blaster was my third game jam game and it was made for FGJ 2023.

The idea of Oink Oink Blaster was that it was just a cute looking Doom game.

I made the enemies and HUD for Oink Oink Blaster.

This was also my first time using Godot as a game engine.

Enemies


As Godot noob it was fun and pretty time consuming to make the enemies.

The enemies are just simple billboards who move randomly and shoot player if it is in their field of view.

UI


I also tried how to do UI in godot.

It didn't work is I would have liked but I got it to work.

MadGoat

Released: Itch.io

Platform: Pc

Engine: Unity

Time: 48hr (2023)


MadGoat was my second game jam game and it was made for KekriJam 2023.

The idea of MadGoat was that you play as the burning Kekri goat and you must finish the level before you burnout.

KekriJam 2023 was a Jam hosted by Kajaani IDGA for a traditional festival that is held in Kajaani named kekri.

Overwiev


I made the burning script and also the lighting for the objects.

It was also my first time using lights in 2D games.

Iron Mice From Hell

Released: Itch.io

Platform: Pc

Engine: Unity

Time: 7 days (2023)


Iron Mice From Hell was the first game jam I have participated and it was made for MouseJam2023.

MouseJam2023 was a gamejam hosted by Ironmouse & Itch.io.

The idea of Iron Mice From Hell is that you play as the Vtuber Ironmouse and you must protect your cables from pesky rats while you are trying to keep your audience entertained.

We placed 5th out of 40 participants.

UI


This simple instruction menu was the first time I tried animating UI and I think I did a pretty good job.

There is also a score counter & fail screens which I made but don't have any videos of them.

Player


The playable character was a pretty simple with movement and a broom hit actions.
